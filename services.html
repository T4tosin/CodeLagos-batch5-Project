Do you have any gluten-free options?

Our chicken is gluten-free. We’ve got some great sides to pick from; salad, and
our coleslaw are all also gluten-free. If it’s a burger you’re after, we have a gluten free bun option,
just inform your waiter when ordering of your allergies and they will look after you.
